Fiteo — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 June 2026

Fiteo is a calorie and macronutrient tracker for iOS. It is built to keep your data on your device. Fiteo has no account, no sign-in, and no cloud sync. This policy explains the few cases where data leaves your device — always because you asked it to — and what we never collect.

Data stored on your device only

Everything you log stays on your iPhone: meals and their estimated nutrition, weights and body measurements, goals, your chosen meal schedule, and app preferences. This is kept in the app's local storage (Apple's on-device databases). We do not have a server that stores your personal data, and we cannot read it.

Your AI provider key (BYOK)

Fiteo works in "bring your own key" (BYOK) mode: you supply your own API key for the AI provider you choose (Anthropic, Google Gemini, Groq, Mistral, or OpenAI). Your key is stored in the iOS Keychain (encrypted and hardware-backed, accessible only to the app). We never see your key, it is never written to logs, and it is never sent anywhere except, as a standard authorization header, directly from your device to the provider you configured.

What is sent to your AI provider

To estimate a meal's calories and macros, the meal description you type (or dictate) is sent directly from your device to the AI provider you selected, using your key. This is the only content sent for estimation, and only when you submit a meal. That request is then subject to your provider's own privacy policy — providers differ in whether they log or train on inputs, so please review the policy of the provider whose key you use:

  • Anthropic
  • Google (Gemini)
  • Groq
  • Mistral
  • OpenAI

Dictation

If you dictate a meal instead of typing it, Fiteo uses Apple's Speech framework to transcribe your voice. The resulting text is then handled exactly like typed text. Speech recognition is governed by Apple's privacy terms.

Apple Health

If you grant access, Fiteo reads from Apple Health to compute indicators and your energy balance: body weight, body-fat percentage, waist circumference, and energy burned (active and basal). Fiteo writes back only one type of data — your body weight — and only when you change it in the app. Health data never leaves your device: it is not sent to AI providers, not written to logs, and not uploaded anywhere. You control this access at any time in iOS Settings → Health. Deleting Fiteo does not remove samples already saved in Apple Health; manage those in the Health app.

Food database

Fiteo ships with an offline food database derived from Open Food Facts (licensed under the ODbL). Lookups run entirely on your device — no food queries are sent over the network.

Analytics

Fiteo uses TelemetryDeck for privacy-preserving, aggregate usage analytics — anonymous event names only (for example, that a screen was opened). It does not collect your meals, weights, health data, API key, name, email, or any identifier that points back to you. You can turn analytics off completely in the app's settings; the opt-out is honored immediately. Fiteo uses no advertising, attribution, or third-party tracking SDKs.

What we do not collect

  • No account, email, or password.
  • No personal data on any Fiteo server (there isn't one for your data).
  • No selling or sharing of your data with advertisers or data brokers.
  • No location tracking, no advertising identifiers.

Children

Fiteo is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Deleting your data

Because your data lives on your device, deleting the app removes the meals, measurements, goals, and preferences stored by Fiteo, and removes your API key from the Keychain. Data you previously wrote to Apple Health remains in Apple Health until you delete it there.
