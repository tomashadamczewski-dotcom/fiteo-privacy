Fiteo: Calories In & Out · iPhone (iOS 17+)

Fiteo is a calorie and macronutrient tracker. You describe a meal in plain language — by typing or speaking — and Fiteo estimates its calories and macros. It also shows your energy balance (calories in vs. out) using Apple Health. No account or sign-in is required.

Contact us

Questions, problems, or feedback? Email fiteoai@gmail.com and we'll get back to you. Telling us your iOS version and what you were doing helps us help you faster.

Frequently asked questions

How do I log a meal?

Open the Day tab, type or dictate what you ate (for example, "two fried eggs and a slice of bread"), and send. Fiteo breaks it into items and estimates calories and macros. You can refine any entry in your own words (e.g. "make it a large portion").

What is Fiteo Pro and the free trial?

AI meal estimation is part of Fiteo Pro, an auto-renewable subscription (monthly or annual) with a 7-day free trial. The trial is free; unless you cancel before it ends, it converts to a paid subscription. You can switch between monthly and annual at any time.

How do I cancel or manage my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple. On your iPhone: Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions → Fiteo Pro, then change or cancel. Cancelling keeps access until the end of the current period. Refunds are handled by Apple at reportaproblem.apple.com.

Is my data private?

Your logged data (meals, weight, measurements, goals) stays on your device, and Apple Health data never leaves your device — Fiteo only writes your body weight back if you allow it. To estimate a meal, the description you enter is sent to our processing service and on to a third-party AI provider (currently Google Gemini), whose model generates the result. It is not stored by us and not tied to an account. Full details are in our Privacy Policy.

Do I need an account?

No. Fiteo has no account, sign-in, email, or password.

Which devices are supported?

iPhone on iOS 17 or later. (iPad and Apple Watch may come later.)
